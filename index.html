The State of Peaches

Known affectionately as "The Peach State," Georgia is a state full of Southern hospitality and striking contrasts. Georgia encourages visitors to enjoy a singular fusion of tradition, natural wonder, and contemporary attractiveness with its rich agricultural heritage, varied landscapes, and energetic towns.

Georgia is located in the center of the American South and spans from the tranquil Atlantic coast in the east to the picturesque Appalachian foothills in the north. Travelers come across moss-draped trees, sweeping farmlands, quaint little towns, and vibrant urban areas teeming with innovation and culture. From the cobblestone lanes of Savannah, one of the oldest cities in America, to the civil rights icons of Atlanta, the state capitol and cultural center, Georgia is steeped in history. Every instant provides a glimpse into the essence of the South, and every location has a tale to tell.

Georgia, however, is more than its history. There is enough of adventure and luxury to be had there. Discover world-famous hiking routes, relax on the Golden Isles, eat award-winning food, or see a live performance at one of Atlanta's iconic venues. The Peach State offers warmth, elegance, and a ton of surprises, whether your interests are in culinary adventures, outdoor getaways, or cultural encounters.

Georgia is a location to experience, taste, and remember, not just a destination.
